Mission

Why Vasana exists

The environmental costs of human development are rarely invisible because they don't exist — they're invisible because no one has been paid to look. Vasana is an open research initiative that does exactly that.

The name

What vāsanā means

In Sanskrit philosophy, vāsanā (वासना) refers to the latent impressions left by past actions — subtle traces that persist in the unconscious and quietly shape future behaviour. You cannot see them directly, but they are always present, always influencing.

This is precisely how industrial and technological development affects the natural world. A datacenter built in 2018 leaves carbon, water, and hardware impressions that persist for decades. A fashion supply chain operating today embeds consequences into river systems, soil chemistry, and atmospheric composition that will outlast the garments themselves.

Vasana is named for this idea: that human progress carries latent environmental impressions that must be made visible before they can be addressed.

The problem

What we're measuring, and why it matters

Environmental data is fragmented, often proprietary, and rarely communicated in ways that enable action. Companies disclose selectively. Methodologies vary. Estimates conflict. The result is that even well-intentioned organisations cannot accurately understand their environmental footprint — let alone reduce it.

AI and machine learning now offer tools to aggregate, cross-reference, and model environmental data at a scale and speed that was previously impossible. Vasana uses these tools in service of transparency, not profit.

Every dataset we produce is open. Every methodology is published. Every number comes with its source, its uncertainty range, and its assumptions clearly stated.

Our principles

How we work

Open by default. All research, data, and methodology is published under open licences. Nothing is paywalled. Nothing requires registration to access.

Honest about uncertainty. Where data is incomplete, estimated, or contested, we say so. We do not present confident numbers where confidence is not warranted.

Independent. Vasana accepts no funding from the industries it studies. Research conclusions are never subject to review or approval by corporate partners.

Actionable. Every research project ends with recommendations — for organisations, policymakers, and individuals — that are grounded in the evidence produced.

The roadmap

Where we're going

Project 01 focuses on AI and datacenter infrastructure — the fastest-growing source of new energy demand globally, and one of the least transparently reported. Subsequent projects will examine fast fashion, urban construction, industrial agriculture, and shipping.

The long-term goal is a unified environmental intelligence platform: a living, AI-updated dataset that gives any researcher, journalist, or policymaker instant access to the best available evidence on the environmental cost of human development.
