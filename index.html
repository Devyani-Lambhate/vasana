Our mission

Vasana exists because the environmental cost of human development is real, measurable, and largely invisible. We build open research tools to quantify the latent impressions that industry, technology, and growth leave on the natural world — making that knowledge accessible to researchers, policymakers, and organisations worldwide.

Our approach

Open methodology,
verifiable findings

Vasana follows a reproducible research framework. Every number on this site can be traced back to its primary source. Where data is incomplete or estimated, we say so explicitly.

S.01 Collect primary data from public disclosures, energy filings, and environmental reports
S.02 Model gaps using peer-reviewed methodology with explicit uncertainty bounds
S.03 Cross-validate with independent researchers and publish all assumptions openly
S.04 Update findings quarterly as new disclosure data becomes available
