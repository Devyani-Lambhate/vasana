Vasana The latent impressions

The Sanskrit word vāsanā refers to the latent impressions left by past actions which are invisible, yet shaping everything that follows. Human development works the same way on our planet. "Every industry, technology, and act of development leaves an invisible mark on the living world. Vasana uses AI-powered research to surface, measure, and communicate what we leave behind.

Our mission

Vasana exists because the environmental cost of human development is real, measurable, and largely invisible. We build open research tools to quantify the latent impressions that industry, technology, and growth leave on the natural world, making that knowledge accessible to researchers, policymakers, and organisations worldwide.

Our Approach
01
Identify the sectors or activities that have the significant environmental impact.
 02
Model gaps and methodology using peer-reviewed approach
 03
Cross-validate with independent researchers and publish all assumptions openly
 04
Make our research openly accessible to researchers, policy makers, and the community
